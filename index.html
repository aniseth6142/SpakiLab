The SpakiLab Story

SpakiLab was born from a bold idea shared between two friends — Aniseth and Peace — in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania.

We saw a gap: traditional welding had not yet entered the digital age. Most fabricators were doing things the old way, without design thinking, without modern aesthetics, without engineering precision at the forefront.

So we decided to transform that. SpakiLab exists to bring welding into the modern world — where beautiful structures, engineering skills, and digital design thinking meet at the forge.

We are young. We are hungry. And we are just getting started.
